About Us

Sarong & Lilit 🌻

A hijab production established on 11 January 2020. Our company produces a variety of the latest design of hijab. We are very concerned about the comfort of the users when wearing our product, so we decided to use a good quality of fabrics that are comfortable to wear and the prices offered are affordable. Sarong & Lilit has released their first product in their first boutique located in the business area in Kuala Lumpur. The incredible response from customers about our product has had a huge impact on Sarong&Lilit to continue to expand the company's empire by producing more hijab collections with a better-quality of hijab. Now, Sarong & Lilit also produces the various kinds and fashion of hijab for women and teenagers.
